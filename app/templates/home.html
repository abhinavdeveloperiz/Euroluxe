THE SPIRIT BEHIND THE SPIRIT

In the shadows of personal tragedy, Lal Karinkada chose not comfort, but courage. After a life-altering injury during the global pandemic and a devastating accident in 2022, he was left with a shattered shoulder and a permanently impaired right hand.Yet, in his lowest moment, a simple "Pappa, I love you" from his son lit a fire within

That fire became Thuruppu Gulan — a brand born not from ease, but from extraordinary resilience. Named after the trump card in Indian tradition, it symbolizes strength when it matters most. Over five relentless months, Lal journeyed across 15 countries to craft spirits that would stand as a testament to survival, revival, and victory. Today, Thuruppu Gulan is more than a premium spirit — it is a declaration that no matter how hard life strikes, we are born to win.
