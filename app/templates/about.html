Our Story

Founded in the vibrant Emirate of Fujairah in 2024, we emerge with a singular ambition: to deliver bold, premium spirits that rise above the ordinary and resonate with distinction.

Backed by Euromax Spirits, Austria, our operations are rooted in legacy, craftsmanship, and an uncompromising commitment to quality. Every label we launch is a testament to precision distillation, global sourcing, and refined taste.

The Spirit Behind The Spirit

In the shadows of personal tragedy, Lal Karinkada chose not comfort, but courage. After a life-altering injury during the global pandemic and a devastating accident in 2022, he was left with a shattered shoulder and an impaired right hand. Yet, in his lowest moment, a simple “Pappa, I love you” from his son lit a fire within.

That fire became Thuruppu Gulan — a brand born not from ease, but extraordinary resilience. Named after the trump card in Indian tradition, it symbolizes strength when it matters most. Over five relentless months, Lal journeyed across 15 countries to craft spirits that would stand as a testament to survival, revival, and victory. Born To Win.

Vision

Thuruppu Gulan is more than a premium spirit — it is a symbol of resilience, revival, and reinvention. Named after the “Trump Card” in Indian tradition, the brand represents the ultimate comeback. No matter the odds, We Are Born To Win.
